Site Purpose

The purpose of the Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce website is that it serves as a central online hub for information, resources, and support for businesses, community members, and other stakeholders.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events and networking opportunities are available?
  • How can businesses and community members get involved in events?
  • How does the Chamber contribute to the community?
  • Who are the key leaders and staff members?
